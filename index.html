Hey, welcome to Poly-Tool - An app that allows you to make geometric art based on polygons!

It's surprising how much you can do with this simple geometry...

Check out these presets to see what Poly-Tool can do...



Cool huh?

Now, head over to the "Poly" tab and make something unique! Preset 0 is a great starting point, which will show you what Poly-Tool does.



Btw, Poly-Tool is going to work best on your PC. Your phone may have a hard time crunching all these big numbers.

If you think what you made is dope you can download your image using this button, and definitely go check out that coffee tab...




*This may or may not work on mobile. Sorry!.
